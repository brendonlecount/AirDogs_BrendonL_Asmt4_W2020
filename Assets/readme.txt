Air Dogs

by Brendon LeCount


Objective
-----------

Fight off the invading airship before it can destroy all of the towns on the island. Enemy biplanes are highlighted in red, while friendlies are highlighted in green. Load the Island Defense scene to play.


Controls
-----------

Mouse - Pitch and yaw

Scroll Wheel - Throttle up/down (8 clicks to full throttle)

Left Mouse Button - Fire machine gun

Right Mouse Button - Toggle first/third person (click) or orbit cam (hold)

Space - Drop bomb (5 second reload time, can be held down to drop repeatedly)
